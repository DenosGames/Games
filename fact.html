Font And Character Test / Copy

This program copies special symbols to the Windows clipboard.
Move your mouse over a symbol to see its name and description.
If this is the symbol you want, just click it and it will be copied to the Windows clipboard.
The default font used for the symbols is "Georgia". Change it via the dropdown.
The text area shows alphanumeric text, pseudo Latin text, or your own text including symbols that you clicked. Click Copy Text to copy your text to the clipboard and save it.
Click Clear Text to erase the saved text in the textarea.
→ Click anywhere in this box to close the Help or wait 15 seconds.
